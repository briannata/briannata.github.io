Hi, I'm Brianna Ta

I'm a senior at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. In my future studies, I plan on majoring in computer science and furthering my research on lie detection. I participate in many clubs, such as Tomorrow's Women in Science and Technology (TWIST), Future Problem Solving, TJ Media, and Public Forum Debate. I'm also a year-round wrestler and Jiu Jitsu practioner. As a volunteer, I tutor for Teach for Care and taught physics to middle schoolers over the summer. Last summer, I was an extern for the Cisco High Program and had the opportunity to go to illuminating workshops and work with professional mentors on my Capstone project.
